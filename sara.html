Solutions And method

The solution to the problem of muscular dystrophy that astronauts suffer from is to provide sports equipment to exersice and activate the muscles daily To develop sports equipments and make them more useful, we put (piezoceramic) that is the most important types of piezoelectric which is a natural physical phenomenon exhibited by some materials, particularly crystals such as quartz and some types of ceramics which have the ability to generate an electric potential difference when subjected to mechanical stress. Whereas, if the surface of the material is strongly pressed, a separation of electrical charges occurs across the crystal lattice of the material, and as a result of the separation of charges, an electrical potential difference is produced on both ends of the material. We used piezoceramic in particular because it is the highest in quality and lowest in price.

Since spaceships need large thrust forces to increase their speed, we decided to use plasma for two reasons The first reason: increasing the speed by relying on the radio waves coming from the sun (sustainable and clean energy) to turn neon or argon gas into plasma Magnets would then push the plasma out as thrust. The second reason : using it instead of nuclear energy because the nuclear energy is a toxic

there is another way_to increase the speed of spaceships_ by using solar sail which is large, thin sheets of plastic attached to a spacecraft and designed so that sunlight can push on them. The process depends on the idea of ​​​​resisting Earth's gravity by taking advantage of the sun's gentle rays, and the idea depends on reflecting the photons of light falling on special plates, which generates movement in this plate according to Newton's third law in the opposite direction

Third:Harmful radiation comes from two main sources in space; low-energy protons emitted from the sun, known as the solar wind, and much higher energy particles known as galactic cosmic rays that originate outside the solar system. The exposure to galactic cosmic rays and solar particles can lead to a significantly higher risk of developing cancer, it can also cause cataracts and possibly Alzheimer's. when these particles knock into the atoms of aluminum that make up a spacecraft hull , their nuclei blow up , emitting yet more superfast particles called secondary radiation .
